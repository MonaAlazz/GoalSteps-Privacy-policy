Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how GoalSteps, developed by Mona Alazzani, handles user data. Our priority is to provide a safe and private experience for everyone using the app.

1. Data Collection

No personal data is collected. GoalSteps does not require you to create an account, sign in, or share any personal information. All goals and habits you track remain stored locally on your device only.

2. Local Data Storage

Your progress, goals, and habits are saved directly on your device. We do not have access to or store this information on our servers.

3. Use of Third-Party Services

To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases, GoalSteps uses RevenueCat. RevenueCat handles purchase validation and may retain purchase-related information in accordance with their privacy policy. For details, please see the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

4. Advertisements

GoalSteps does not use third-party advertising services. No advertising IDs or tracking data are collected.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains under your control.

6. Data Security

Since data is stored only on your device, its security depends on your device’s protection. We recommend enabling device security features such as passcodes or biometric locks.

7. Children’s Privacy

GoalSteps is suitable for all ages. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise children’s use when necessary. No personal data is collected from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements or regulatory changes. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page will always indicate the latest version.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: newmonaalazz@gmail.com
